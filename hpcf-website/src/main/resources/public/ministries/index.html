Our Ministries

Find your place to serve, grow, and connect with others.

Men's Ministry

The Men’s Ministry at Higher Praise is dedicated to building strong men of God who lead their families and communities with integrity. We provide a space for men to be transparent, accountable, and encouraged.

Ministry Focus

  • Biblical Manhood & Leadership
  • Brotherhood & Accountability
  • Service Projects & Community Outreach

Get Involved

We meet monthly for prayer breakfasts and fellowship. Join us for our next gathering!

Women's Ministry

Our Women’s Ministry creates a supportive environment where women can grow in their faith, find healing, and build lasting friendships. We believe in empowering women to walk in their God-given purpose.

Ministry Focus

  • Spiritual Growth & Discipleship
  • Mentorship & Support
  • Annual Retreats & Conferences

Youth Ministry (HYPE)

Higher Praise Youth Empowerment (HYPE) is all about raising up the next generation to be passionate followers of Christ. We provide a safe, fun, and relevant atmosphere for teens to encounter God.

Ministry Focus

  • Biblical Teaching relevant to teen culture
  • Developing Identity in Christ
  • Fun, Food, and Fellowship

Children's Ministry

We believe in planting seeds of faith early. Our Children's Ministry provides a safe, engaging, and Christ-centered environment where kids can learn about God's love through stories, crafts, and play.

Ministry Focus

  • Biblical Foundations
  • Creative Learning & Crafts
  • Fun & Fellowship

Media Ministry

Our Media Ministry team works behind the scenes to ensure that the message of Christ is heard and seen clearly. From sound engineering and livestreaming to graphic design and video production, we use technology to amplify the worship experience.

Ministry Focus

  • Live Streaming & Video Production
  • Audio Engineering & Sound
  • Social Media & Graphic Design

Worship & Arts

The Worship & Arts ministry leads the congregation into God's presence through music, song, and creative expression. We believe worship is a lifestyle, not just a song.

Ministry Focus

  • Praise Team & Choir
  • Musicians & Band
  • Cultivating an Atmosphere of Worship

Outreach & Missions

We are committed to being the hands and feet of Jesus. Our Outreach ministry focuses on serving our local community in La Mesa and sharing the love of Christ through practical acts of kindness.

Ministry Focus

  • Community Food Drives
  • Street Evangelism
  • Partnering with Local Shelters

Ushers & Greeters

The Ushers and Greeters are the first faces you see at Higher Praise. Dedicated to maintaining order and extending hospitality, they ensure everyone feels welcome and comfortable.

Ministry Focus

  • Hospitality & Welcoming
  • Service Order & Assistance
  • Safety & Comfort

Intercessory Prayer

Our Intercessory Prayer team stands in the gap, lifting up the needs of the church, the community, and the world. We believe that prayer changes things and is the foundation of our ministry.

Ministry Focus

  • Spiritual Warfare
  • Healing & Deliverance
  • Covering the Church Leadership

Higher Praise Christian Fellowship

“You will never walk alone at Higher Praise Christian Fellowship.” No one gets left behind. Our mission is to reconcile the lost to Christ.
